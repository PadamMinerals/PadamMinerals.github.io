About Us

30+ Years

Experience

Padam Group

Padam Group established in the year 1985, Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. We began our venture as the wholesale supplier of talc powder, silica lumps and a lot more.

Our primary commitment lies in the development and design of indigenous products that serve to enhance cost-efficiency measures for our valued customers, thereby ensuring their utmost satisfaction with their investments. Our products offer longevity, durability, extended lifespans, superior energy efficiency, and various other advantages, enabling our clients to economize on expenses associated with conventional product repairs, resulting in significant savings of both time and resources.

Our organization maintains regular communication with industry leaders and clientele, actively seeking insights into their evolving needs and requirements. We are committed to anticipatory responses, ensuring the fulfillment of all demands.
